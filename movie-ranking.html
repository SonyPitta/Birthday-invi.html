My top 3 movies of all time.

Ninu kori

Ninnu Kori is a romantic drama film in Telugu starring Nani, Nivetha Thomas, and Aadhi Pinisetty. The movie explores themes of love, heartbreak, and second chances as it follows the story of Uma and Pallavi, whose love story takes unexpected turns. With emotional depth, captivating performances, and soulful music, the film delivers a heart-touching narrative.

Raja movie

Raja is a heartwarming Telugu movie about a kind guy named Raja. He sacrifices his love for a friend but ends up marrying the same girl under unexpected circumstances. The story beautifully shows how their relationship grows and deals with emotions and sacrifices.

Sankranthi movie

It’s such a beautiful family movie. The story is about this guy who holds his joint family together, but things start to change when his brothers get married, and their priorities shift. It’s all about relationships, sacrifices, and the importance of family. The emotions feel so real, and the ending is so satisfying. You’ll love it!.
